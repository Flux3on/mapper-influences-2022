Mapper Influences

In May 2018, most mappers with ranked maps were asked for the names of the three mappers who most influenced their mapping. This website serves to show the results of that question!

In the box below, type the name of any mapper to see who they consider personally influential. "Mentions" refers to another user including a user as one of their influences. In the chart (accessible from the upper menu), visually see how those connections are formed. In the stats page, see some of the more interesting general aspects of the results.
